**2. WHO WE ARE**

Oxtra is a technology-driven company dedicated to advancing sustainability e-transportation solutions in Africa. Oxtra provides a peer-to-peer online car sharing platform that connects car owners who wish to rent out their cars and individuals seeking to rent a Car. Oxtra is an intermediary between Vehicle Owners and Renters.

We do not subject data subjects to automated decision making on our platforms. User ratings will be based strictly on ratings issued by fellow users after the conclusion of a transaction, or based on our ratings issued based on User conduct or verified complaints.

**6. INFORMATION WE COLLECT ABOUT YOU**

Personal data means any information about an individual from which such a person can be identified either directly or indirectly. We may collect, use, store and transfer different kinds of personal data about you which we have grouped together as follows:

- **Identity Data:** When you create an account, we collect your name, age, gender, email address, and also profile information from the connected social media accounts (Facebook, Google or Email addresses). We may also collect your identification card, title, date of birth and gender, professional background, and social media account handle. You will have a public profile page which will be visible to the public, and will include your name, gender, city, biography, and profile images.
- **Contact Data:** Includes home address, country of residence, billing address, delivery address, email address and telephone numbers.
- **Technical Data:** Includes internet protocol (IP) address, your login data, browser type and version, time zone setting and location, browser plug-in types and versions, services viewed, posts liked or interacted with, operating system and platform, and other technology on the devices you use to access this website.
- **Location Information:** We collect and process your location data when you login to our platform to provide you with relevant services, such as finding nearby rental cars and facilitating the seamless completion of the transaction. We will also collect information about trip locations during the period when a User rents a Vehicle.
- **Rental Information:** We collect details about your car rental transactions, including pickup and drop-off locations, rental duration, and payment information.
- **Identity verification data:** Being a high-risk service, in some instances, we may collect information for identity verification such as a photograph or scanned copy of a driver’s license, national ID card, passport, payment card, social media account information, or other forms of identification information.
- **Vehicle information:** When you intend to list a vehicle on the platform, we will collect information in relation to such vehicle which you want to list as available for rent. This will include vehicle model, make and year, vehicle identification number (VIN), availability dates, reviews, and colored vehicle photos.

We use cookies, web beacons, and other tracking technologies on some parts of our website and mobile devices to remember your preferences and settings, enable you to log in seamlessly, track rented vehicles, provide targeted advertising, combat fraud, analyze our platforms’ performance, and fulfill other legitimate interests such as improving our platforms. You can stop your browser or mobile device from accepting cookies, but if you do, some parts of our website or online services may not work appropriately. Please read our comprehensive cookies policy for more on this section.

**9. WHO DO WE SHARE YOUR PERSONAL DATA WITH?**

We do not share your personal information with any third parties. We will disclose your Vehicle listings, availability dates and ratings to enable Users to make informed decisions.

Oxtra will also disclose other personal information such as your mobile phone number, and location of the vehicle as reasonably necessary to facilitate each transaction.

We may disclose your personal information to regulatory authorities only as provided by law, and with IT system providers and platform providers that help us host our application and manage our email services on the grounds of legitimate interest.
